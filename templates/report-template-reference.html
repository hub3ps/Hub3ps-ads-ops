What We Did
Optimizations Implemented
Below is a detailed summary of every optimization made to your campaigns this period. Each change was carefully analysed before being implemented.
⌧
Paused keywords outside campaign scope
We found two keywords — "orthodontist clinic" and "denture clinic" — that were attracting people looking for services not included in the current campaigns (orthodontics and dentures are advertised by other clinics in the group). These were paused to prevent paying for clicks that can't convert into appointments for the advertised services.
NaeNae [PSEO] ✓ Done
✎
Removed out-of-scope ad headlines
Two headlines in your main ad were referencing "all on x clinic" and "dental implants" — services not included in the current campaigns (implants are advertised by another clinic in the group). These were removed to ensure your ads only promote the services included in the current campaigns.
NaeNae [PSEO] ✓ Done
✕
Blocked 12 emergency-related search terms
The Preventive campaign was receiving clicks from people searching for emergency services (e.g., "urgent dentist", "toothache", "broken tooth"). Since this campaign focuses on routine and preventive care, we blocked 12 emergency-related terms to keep the budget focused on the right audience.
Upper Hutt Preventive ✓ Done
✕
Created competitor blocking list
We set up a shared list of competitor names — Art of Dentistry, Gentle Dental, Bee Healthy, Flosii, Lumino — and applied it to both campaigns. This prevents your ads from showing when people are specifically searching for other dental clinics, saving budget for patients who are open to choosing a provider.
Both campaigns ✓ Done
✕
Blocked irrelevant search terms
Searches for "veneers", "crown", "dental hygienists", and "hutt city" were triggering ads on the Preventive campaign without converting. These terms were added as negatives to focus the budget on relevant searches.
Upper Hutt Preventive ✓ Done
✕
Applied standard negative keywords list (151 terms)
We applied our comprehensive negative keywords list to the Preventive campaign. This list contains 151 commonly irrelevant terms for dental clinics (such as "jobs", "salary", "study", "free", etc.) and prevents your ads from showing for searches that will never result in a patient booking.
Upper Hutt Preventive ✓ Done
◷
Optimised ad schedule
We paused ad delivery between midnight and 5 AM for the Preventive campaign. Data showed that clicks during these hours were not converting into appointments. This redirects that budget into daytime hours when patients are more likely to book.
Upper Hutt Preventive ✓ Done
⚙
Consolidated negative keywords structure
Emergency-related negatives were scattered across individual ad groups. We consolidated them to the campaign level so they apply uniformly, making management cleaner and ensuring no ad group accidentally receives irrelevant emergency traffic.
Upper Hutt Preventive ✓ Done
⚙
Removed unused ad groups
Three legacy ad groups — Restorative Dentistry, Dental Bridges, and Dentures — had been sitting inactive with no spend. We removed them to keep the campaign structure clean and focused.
Upper Hutt Preventive ✓ Done
